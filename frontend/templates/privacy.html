Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

SpeakFit ("we", "our", or "us") is a speech analysis and coaching application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile app and web services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (from Google Sign-In)
  • Display name
  • Profile picture URL

Audio Recordings

  • Voice recordings you create within the app
  • Transcripts generated from your recordings
  • Speech analysis results (pace, clarity, filler words, etc.)

Usage Data

  • App version and device type
  • Features used and session duration
  • Error reports (to improve the app)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: Analyze your speech and provide coaching feedback
  • Improve your experience: Track your progress over time
  • AI Coaching: Power the Speech Coach feature with context from your recordings
  • Model training: Use recordings to train and improve our speech analysis models (Pro users can opt out)
  • App improvement: Fix bugs and develop new features
  • Communication: Send important updates about the service

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • User accounts and metadata: Encrypted PostgreSQL database
  • Audio files: Encrypted cloud storage (Cloudflare R2)
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Authentication via Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting to operate the service
  • Authentication: Google Sign-In for account access
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your recordings and data in the app
  • Delete: Delete individual recordings or your entire account
  • Export: Download your data (contact us for full export)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in app settings

7. Children's Privacy

SpeakFit includes a Kids Mode designed for children learning to read. For users under 13, we recommend parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's data.

8. Data Retention & Model Training

We retain your data indefinitely to provide and improve our services. By using SpeakFit, you agree that your voice recordings and transcripts may be used to train and improve our speech analysis models.

Please note: While we strive to maintain reliable service, your data may become unavailable at any time due to technical issues, service changes, or other factors. We recommend keeping your own copies of important recordings.

Pro Users: SpeakFit Pro subscribers can opt out of model training in Settings. Your recordings will still be stored to provide the service, but will not be used to train our models.

When you delete a recording, it is removed from your account. When you delete your account, your personal information is removed, though anonymized data may be retained for model training (unless you opted out as a Pro user).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
